One of the first briefs of my Master's programme was to create a 30-second video about a topic of my choosing while including visual effects, motion graphics, and 3D elements. I ended up choosing Uno because it was a game that I had played a lot and I thought the rules could fit into the time allowance. I gave it a 70's theme because it was the era when the game came out.
