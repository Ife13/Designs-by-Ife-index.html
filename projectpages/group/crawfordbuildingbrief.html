To ease us into fourth year, we started with a group project. The brief was to redesign DJCAD's Crawford building to be more inviting to the public and more of a reflection of what goes on within it. My group, was thinking about the stereotypes associated with art school and how the students can be seen as 'freaky', so we reclaimed the word to create a vibrant building inspired by the circus and fun fairs.
